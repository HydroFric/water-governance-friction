About HydroFric

📌 Purpose & Scope – For rural water supply

The mission of HydroFric is to help rural water service providers diagnose the most urgent gaps between policy promises, available resources, on‑ground infrastructure, and what families actually experience.

🧠 Conceptual Framework & Methodology

HydroFric is grounded in the Socio‑Technical Friction (STF) Framework. Users score four domains (P, I, E, H) from 1 to 5. The tool calculates friction and provides pattern‑specific diagnosis and intervention.

Show methodology details

Friction is calculated as the sum of squared gaps: F = (P-I)² + (I-E)² + (E-H)² + (P-H)². Larger gaps are penalised more heavily.
